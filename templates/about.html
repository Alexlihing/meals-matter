Who we are

Every day, tons of perfectly good food are thrown away, while many communities are still left in need. MealsMatter is more than just a food-sharing platform. We're a community of leaders that strive to take on one of the most overlooked problems in our communities.

We partner with grocery stores, bakeries, and restaurants to ensure quality foods are delivered to the hands that need it, reducing waste and fostering supportive communities. Interested in being a part of the change? Join us in making a difference, one meal at a time.
